Infinite Consciousness

I Come from The Past And The Future …

When I woke up, I had no idea how many earth years have passed …

I used to be a maritime adventurer. In 1500 AD, I set off on an adventure with a group of 20 seafaring enthusiasts. We were heading for the New World, which is now the American West. We planned to sail for 101 days, and then we would arrive on this land. I could barely contain my excitement to discover this unknown and mysterious land.

Unfortunately, hurricane weather set us back, sending us off course where we lost direction.

We drifted for days, almost running out of food and staring at the possibility of starvation. It was at this time that we spotted land and immediately went to dock. Once ashore, we went in search of food. Alas, the island was desolate. With no other option, we chose to stay on the island.

I slowly watched as I lost my companions one after the other until I was the only one left hanging on. I found a cave on the island and pushed my starving body to walk towards it. As I was beginning to lost consciousness, I heard a deep voice pulling me in from the depths of the cave …

I stayed awake, slowly walking into the deep cave. I saw an unidentified object with shining laser light. Curious to see what it was, I picked it up. It was a novelty, and I had never seen anything like it before. As I examined it closely, I noticed it was shaped like a soldier’s metal helmet. Instinctively, I tried it on. Immediately I felt dizzy! Like my soul was being pulled out of my body, moving at incredible speed through another time and space.

When I regained consciousness, I was in unknown territory – a place of endless night. It was like being on Earth with no greenery or vitality. I was surprised to find I could not touch my own body, and I had become a virtual object. Using my mind, I could move to anywhere I wanted to be. It was an amazing experience!

I moved forward for about two miles and encountered a creature I had never seen before. I went closer to say hello to the creature, but I found it unable to make a sound. I realized that this alien creature seemed to know what I was thinking. Through our consciousnesses, we had a conversation that went something like this …

“Hello, I’m glad you are finally here, Simon. Let me briefly introduce myself. I’m a member of planet KP 118. You can call me N.O. 9588.”

Then he told me, “In 2021 AD, human beings sent a detector into space from Earth, which members from KP 118 captured. Human beings are a mass of consciousness controlled by aliens from another advanced civilization, planet OJ 414. The existence of this civilization is invisible to Earthlings.

The aliens created a virtual composite vision of the human brain, manipulating human minds to obtain information about the Earth. They plan to seize the Earth’s resources after 2577 AD and finally destroy all humans. We want to help Earthlings escape from their control so that human beings can join us in the fight against OJ 414, which is our rival planet, and they want to invade our world too.”

Finally, N.O. 9588 sent me back to Earth through the wormhole…

And I was given the mission to save humanity, free from alien control, and to restore human infinite consciousness.
